About Me

Hello! My name is Mia Raupach, and I am studying environmental science and musical theatre at DVC. I love learning about all types of things to round out my knowledge of the world, and I love creating art that reflects my love for the world. I want this website to be a space for me to show off my accomplishments through my hobbies. It started as a project for my AP Computer Science Principles Class, but it has grown into so much more. <3

I've always considered myself to be a bit of a jack of all trades. I love trying new things all the time! I play the drums and other percussive instruments, I draw, I write, and so so much more. I created this website as a bit of a portfolio for all things me, but I think it's so fun that it will never incapsulate all of me!
